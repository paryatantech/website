AI-Powered Ancillary Revenue

Transform every booking into a revenue opportunity. Upsell.ai is an automated upselling engine that engages guests before they arrive with hyper-personalized offers they can’t resist. From room upgrades to experiences, watch your ancillary revenue soar without lifting a finger.

Key Features:

  • AI-Powered Targeting: Our algorithm analyzes guest data to send the right offer at the right time.
  • Fully Automated: Set it up once and let our system do the work 24/7.
  • WhatsApp & Email Delivery: Reach guests on the platforms they use most.
  • Dynamic Pricing: Maximize revenue with intelligent pricing suggestions.

ReviewReply.ai

Automated Reputation Management

Your online reputation is your most valuable asset. Stop spending 10+ hours a week manually responding to reviews. Our AI scrapes every major platform, understands the sentiment, and drafts perfect, personalized replies in your brand's unique voice. You just click 'Approve'.

Key Features:

  • Multi-Platform Scraping: Gathers reviews from Google, Booking.com, Tripadvisor, and more.
  • Sentiment Analysis: Intelligently detects positive and negative feedback to tailor responses.
  • Brand Voice Customization: Learns your communication style for authentic replies.
  • One-Click Approval Dashboard: Quickly approve or edit dozens of replies in minutes.

TipTap.qr

Contactless Tipping & Feedback

Happy staff create happy guests. Empower your guests to show appreciation and provide valuable feedback instantly. A simple QR code scan lets guests tip your staff directly using UPI, cards, or wallets, and leave private feedback before it ever becomes a negative online review.

Key Features:

  • Instant & Cashless: Secure digital tipping directly to your staff's bank accounts.
  • Real-Time Feedback: Get immediate insights on service quality to address issues proactively.
  • Performance Analytics: Track staff performance and guest satisfaction trends.
  • Easy to Deploy: Simple QR code stickers can be placed anywhere in your property.
